How To Make Pie Dough
  • shopping_cartIngredients
    • add_circle_outline2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
    • add_circle_outline1 cup unsalted butter - chilled, cut into tablespoon-size pieces
    • add_circle_outline2 teaspoon salt
    • add_circle_outline7 tablespoons ice water
    • add_circle_outline1 tablespoon cider vinegar
    • add_circle_outlineAdd all ingredients to list
  • cakeDirections
    1. Combine flour, salt, and butter in a food processor. Pulse until mixture resembles coarse crumbs, about 10 1-second pulses.
    2. Stir water and vinegar in a small bowl.
    3. Pour half the ice water and vinegar mixture into the flour and butter mixture. Pulse to combine, about 3 (1-second) pulses.
    4. Pour in remaining ice water and vinegar mixture. Pulse until mixture just starts to come together, about 8 (1-second) pulses.
    5. Turn dough out onto a wooden surface, pat into round shape and divide in half. Form each half into a disc about 5 inches wide.
    6. Wrap each disc in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes until ready to use.
